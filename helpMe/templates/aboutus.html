Who we are and
what we do

helpMe basically allows the users to learn new things or get their jobs done with the help of skilled people around them who are experts in their particular fields. Bringing people together and providing the services they want immediately will reduce their time searching through other resources and will also create a network of people and skills around them. With the help of this not only the people getting help will be benefited but also the people providing the help would be able to utilize their skills. For instance, they would be able monetize their skills during their availability times.

This is a collaborative platform of help seeker and provider where the seeker posts the type of help needed and contacts the provider accordingly. Both of them discuss their agenda, meeting times, areas and other things. The website takes input such as personal information, skills, availability etc. It is a one-step resolution of all our specialized queries requiring the assistance of the expert. This website is not only limited to particular field such as technical difficulties but also to other fields such as sports, arts, business, nursing, law etc.
